I'm a writer, teacher, musician, and web developer from Austin, TX. As a writer, my work has ranged from peer-reviewed articles and a book (currently under consideration) to industry journalism focused on augmented and virtual reality. As a developer, I've devised and collaboratively built websites, which include a voice-activated presentation tool that controls Google Slides via custom keywords, an interactive map that tracks listeners across Spotify's 4,000 genres, and (in my current project) a topic and sentiment analyzer that uses natural language processing to visualize news coverage. In addition to my writing and coding, I'm a teacher at Austin Community College and a musician in the Austin band Hidden Ritual.
